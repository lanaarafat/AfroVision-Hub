WHAT WE DO

Empowerment Programs

Offer workshops, seminars, and training sessions to equip African Animation, VFX & Gaming professionals with advocacy skills. These programs aim to educate professionals on industry challenges, policy issues, and effective strategies for advocating for improved trading conditions.

Resource Hub

Provide a comprehensive repository of research reports, market analyses, and policy briefs related to the animation, VFX, and gaming sectors. This resource hub serves as a knowledge base to inform advocacy efforts and empower professionals with accurate data and insights.

Collaboration Opportunities

Facilitate networking events, forums, and industry conferences to foster collaboration among professionals, industry associations, and policymakers. By bringing together diverse stakeholders, these platforms encourage dialogue, idea exchange, and collective action to address common challenges and advocate for better trading conditions.

Promotion and Distribution

Raise awareness about the importance of favorable trading conditions for the animation industry through targeted campaigns, social media advocacy, and industry publications. By highlighting success stories, case studies, and the economic benefits of a thriving animation sector, professionals can garner support and momentum for advocacy efforts.
